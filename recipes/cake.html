Cake

Main Page

Description

Cake is a popular dessert enjoyed around the world, known for its soft texture and sweet flavor. It is typically made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, then baked until light and fluffy, often layered or topped with frosting.

Cakes are commonly served at celebrations such as birthdays, weddings, and holidays, symbolizing joy and togetherness. With countless varieties—from simple sponge cakes to rich chocolate and fruit-filled creations—cake remains a timeless treat loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk, and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 25–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.
