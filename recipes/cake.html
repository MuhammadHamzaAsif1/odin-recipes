Cake

Description

Cake is a popular dessert enjoyed around the world, known for its soft texture and sweet flavor. It is typically made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, then baked until light and fluffy, often layered or topped with frosting.

Cakes are commonly served at celebrations such as birthdays, weddings, and holidays, symbolizing joy and togetherness. With countless varieties—from simple sponge cakes to rich chocolate and fruit-filled creations—cake remains a timeless treat loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
